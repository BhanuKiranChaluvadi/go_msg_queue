## The Role

We are looking for an engineer passionate about building the next generation of high-scale audio processing infrastructure for communication centers that form a vital part of our critical infrastructure, including emergency dispatch centers and hospitals. You will be responsible for integrating AI capabilities into environments dominated by network protocols like SIP and SIPREC, ensuring robust and scalable performance at the heart of life-critical operations.

### What You'll Be Doing

- Architect and maintain systems processing over 100,000 calls per day
- Develop foundational infrastructure using network protocols such as SIP and SIPREC
- Integrate AI capabilities into low-latency, high-throughput audio processing pipelines
- Collaborate with the Platform Engineering team with a commitment to engineering excellence to ensure system reliability and scalability
- Hands-on development of an innovative solution where design choices matter
- Work with colleagues in design and code reviews
- Contribute to a strong culture of development at Corti through mentorship of developers of all levels

### What You Bring

**Required:**
- Master of Golang
- Deep understanding of network protocols, specifically SIP and SIPREC
- Proven track record of building and scaling foundational infrastructure
- Experience in ideating and implementing features with high complexity
- Hands-on experience with cloud computing architecture and development
- Experience running, monitoring, and operating REST, WebSockets, Kafka or an event-driven architecture
- Experience with containers and orchestration tools
- A passion for writing clean and efficient code
- Excellent English verbal and written communication skills, including all-remote communication

**Significant Plus:**
- Experience with WebSocket, gRPC and CockroachDB
- Experience with real-time audio processing and WebRTC
